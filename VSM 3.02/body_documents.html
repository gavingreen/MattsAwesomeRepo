The documents that accompany EPM2 are catalogued in this section by function. There is an alphabetical index available, should you know the name of the document you seek.

Deliverables
Documents that are part of the project and require either customer approval or are integral to the process of building the solution.

Supporting Documents
Used to gather information, keep records of some type or to perform analysis of some aspect of the project. Some of these may be optional and others may be replaced in some projects by dedicated applications used for logging the information.

Index
The alphabetical listing of all the files that accompany EPM2.
