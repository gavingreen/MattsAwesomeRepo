Welcome to EMC Solution Methods 3.0

The EMC Solution Methods (EPM2) is a framework that helps EMC customers,  consultants, and partners develop EMC e-business solutions as quickly and successfully as possible. Based on the extensive project experience of EMC Professional Services (EPS), EPM2 uses flexible, repeatable methods and tools, and is continually improved by incorporating feedback from consultants and projects.

EPM2 life cycle phases currently include planning through operational deployment, with tasks that are adaptable to multiple types of accounts and projects. EPM2 Phases and Tasks are linked to and supported by on-line documentation, guidelines and document stencils to provide users with a full set of methods and tools. These methods and tools accelerate the development process, maximize the return on investment, and reduce the risk of overlooking elements critical to the success of the project.
